Hi! I’m an aspiring software developer, always looking to gain more skills and delve into the depths of the industry. I’m currently a first-year student in Shopify’s Dev Degree program. The Dev Degree program at Shopify takes on 10-15 students per university as interns, and lets them gain industry experience alongside gaining their undergraduate degree in Computer Science. The program is currently held in partnership with Carleton University and York University, the latter of which I attend. Students spend eight months on the training path, and then spend 4 eight-month periods interning on 4 different teams.
